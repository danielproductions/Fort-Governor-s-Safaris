At Fort Governor’s, history intertwines with the heartbeat of the land, crafting an experience that transcends time. Founded by Barbara Kind in 2014, our estate emerged from a vision to revive a sanctuary for both guests and wildlife alike. What began as a restoration project blossomed into a vibrant reserve, showcasing the rich tapestry of South Africa’s natural wonders. Our lodges, born from meticulous renovation, stand as a testament to our commitment to providing a haven where every visitor can forge their own unforgettable story. Since our official launch in 2016, we've welcomed wanderers from every corner of the globe, inviting them to discover the magic that pulses through the heart of the Eastern Cape.
